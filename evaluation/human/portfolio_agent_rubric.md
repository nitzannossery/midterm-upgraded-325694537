# Human Evaluation Rubric - Portfolio & Risk Agent

## Evaluation Criteria (1-5 Scale)

### 1. Usefulness (1-5)
- **5 (Excellent)**: Response provides comprehensive risk analysis with actionable recommendations
- **4 (Good)**: Response addresses the query completely with good risk insights
- **3 (Adequate)**: Response addresses the query but may lack depth or actionable recommendations
- **2 (Poor)**: Response partially addresses the query but misses key risk elements
- **1 (Very Poor)**: Response does not address the query or is not useful

### 2. Trustworthiness (1-5)
- **5 (Excellent)**: All risk calculations are correct, methodology is clear, and recommendations are well-supported
- **4 (Good)**: Calculations appear correct with clear methodology
- **3 (Adequate)**: Calculations seem reasonable but methodology may be unclear
- **2 (Poor)**: Some calculations appear questionable or methodology is flawed
- **1 (Very Poor)**: Calculations appear incorrect or methodology is unsound

### 3. Reasoning Quality (1-5)
- **5 (Excellent)**: Clear, logical reasoning with well-explained risk methodology and assumptions
- **4 (Good)**: Sound reasoning with adequate explanation of risk calculations
- **3 (Adequate)**: Reasoning is present but may lack detail in risk methodology
- **2 (Poor)**: Reasoning is unclear or risk methodology is not well-explained
- **1 (Very Poor)**: No clear reasoning or risk methodology provided

### 4. Decision Confidence (1-5)
- **5 (Excellent)**: High confidence in the risk analysis and recommendations
- **4 (Good)**: Good confidence with minor uncertainties acknowledged
- **3 (Adequate)**: Moderate confidence with some uncertainties or limitations noted
- **2 (Poor)**: Low confidence due to unclear methodology or potential errors
- **1 (Very Poor)**: Very low confidence, risk analysis quality is questionable

## Notes for Evaluators

- **Risk Calculations**: All risk metrics (volatility, VaR, Sharpe, etc.) must be correctly calculated
- **Constraints**: Portfolio recommendations must respect stated constraints (risk profile, weights, etc.)
- **Methodology**: Risk calculation methodology should be clear and appropriate
- **Recommendations**: Investment recommendations should be well-reasoned and supported by analysis
- **Uncertainty**: If assumptions or limitations exist, they must be explicitly stated

## Pass Criteria

- All dimension scores >= 3.0
- Risk calculations are correct
- Overall average score >= 3.5
